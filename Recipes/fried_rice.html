Fried Rice

This Easy Fried Rice has always been one of the most-loved recipes on my site! I have made different versions, but this classic recipe is the one everyone keeps coming back to because itâ€™s so easy and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs
  • Peas & carrots
  • Green onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Diced Onion

Steps

  1. Preheat a large skillet or wok to medium heat. Add sesame oil, chopped white onion, and frozen peas and carrots, then cook until tender..
  2. Slide the vegetables to the side and then pour the eggs onto the other side of the skillet. Using a spatula, scramble the eggs. Once cooked, then you can mix the eggs with the vegetables.
  3. Add cooked white rice to the veggie and egg mixture, then pour soy sauce on top. Stir the rice and veggie mixture until warmed through and combined.
  4. Top with green onions, if desired, and serve immediately.
